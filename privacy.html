Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Canto collects nothing. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, no cloud sync, no account required. Your files stay on your Mac.

Data Collection

Canto is a native macOS application built by Infinite Labs OÜ (Estonia). Canto does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No analytics or usage tracking of any kind
  • No telemetry or crash reporting
  • No network requests are made by the app
  • No account creation required
  • No cloud storage, sync, or backup

File Access

Canto accesses markdown files on your local filesystem solely to provide editing and preview functionality. Files are:

  • Read only when you open them in the editor
  • Written only when you explicitly save
  • Never uploaded or shared with any server or third party

Canto uses macOS App Sandbox for security. File access is limited to folders you explicitly open through the standard file picker or drag-and-drop.

Third-Party Services

Canto does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any external party.

Children's Privacy

Canto is not directed at children under 13. As Canto collects no personal information from any user, no special provisions for minors are necessary.

Changes

If this policy changes, updates will be posted at this URL and reflected in app updates.
